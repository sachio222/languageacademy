Module 3: Être (To Be)

Learn the most fundamental verb in French - the building block for countless expressions

👁️ Concept Introduction

The verb être (to be) is the foundation of French grammar. Before we practice, let's understand the complete pattern. This systematic approach reduces anxiety and helps your brain encode the information more effectively.

Notice how each form has its own unique sound and spelling. French speakers use these forms automatically, but as learners, we benefit from seeing the complete system first.

🧠 Why This Matters

Être appears in countless French expressions. Once you master these six forms, you'll be able to:

• Describe yourself and others: "Je suis professeur"
• Express location: "Nous sommes à Paris"
• Form the passé composé: "Elle est arrivée"
• Create complex sentences with confidence
